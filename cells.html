Cells
Brandon Gioggia

Instructions:

The box at the center of the screen is a grid full of white cells. Click in the box to turn cells on or off. All of the cells start off, and will turn black when they are on.

The buttons on the right side of the screen allow you to set rules for the cells to turn themselves on and off.

The number on each button indicates the number of neighbors a cell has that are on. The command at the bottom of each button indicates what the cell will do when it has that many neighbors set to on. You can toggle through these states by clicking.

Press the 'Play' button to have the cells start following your rules.

If you're unsure where to start, try the following combination:
0:On, 1:Off, 2:On, 3:On, 4:Off
